Here is a comprehensive strategy for your Facebook vertical ads. I noticed you mentioned "vertical 16:9"—I have adjusted this to the correct vertical format for mobile, which is 9:16 (1080x1920 pixels), as this is the standard aspect ratio for Facebook Stories and Reels.

To merge the aesthetics of Apple and Samsung, we will combine Apple’s hyper-realistic, minimalist "monolith" lighting with Samsung’s vibrant, high-contrast cinematic grading and UI elements.

From Apple: We will use Rim/Edge Lighting at a 135° angle and Negative Fill to carve the product out of a pitch-black void. We will also use Focus Stacking to ensure the bags and wood grain have an "infinite depth of field" so every detail is perfectly sharp.

From Samsung: We will apply their "Nightography" Teal and Orange cinematic color grading to make the flames and wood pop against the shadows. We will also integrate "Squircle" (square-circle) UI elements and floating particle effects (like glowing embers) in their signature "Supernova Yellow" or "Cerulean" blue.
