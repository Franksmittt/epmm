The targeting remains hyper-focused on the high-value Land Cruiser Purist within your 50 km operational radius.

3. Maximum-Impact Budgeting: The "Rank ASAP" Local Strategy

The core principle here is to out-target the competition on local intent, which is cheaper than national brand awareness.

Split Test (If Budget Allows): Use a small daily budget (e.g., R250/day) split between two Ad Sets:

Ad Set A (LC79 Purist): Land Cruiser + Old Man Emu + 4x4 Community.

Ad Set B (High-Value Bakkies): Toyota Hilux + Ford Ranger + Ironman 4x4.

The LC79 owner is the least price-sensitive and is the goal of "The Purist" segmentation, so focus most of your energy here.

Monitor Performance: After 48 hours, look for the Ad Set with the lowest Cost Per Message/Call. Immediately reallocate 80% of your remaining budget to the winner.

Frequency Cap: Ensure your campaign is set to optimize for reach with a tight frequency cap (e.g., no more than 3-4 impressions per person per week). This prevents the ad from annoying your hyper-local, high-value audience, which is critical for local trust.

Integration of the Address: By prominently featuring your Alberton address and phone number, the ad legitimately ranks for high-intent, local service-based keywords (e.g., "Land Cruiser Suspension Fitment Pretoria" or "LC79 suspension Alberton") without requiring the website, directly challenging high-trust local specialists.
