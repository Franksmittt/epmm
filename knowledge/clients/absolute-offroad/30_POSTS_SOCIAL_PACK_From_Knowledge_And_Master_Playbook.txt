DAY 4 — THERMAL FADE (WHY SHOCKS “GO OFF”)
================================================================================
Pillar: Education / fear → fix
Format: Feed 1:1 infographic style
Hook: “Heat is the enemy of damping.”
Caption:
Corrugations cycle your shocks fast. Energy becomes heat. Oil thins. Foam
forms. Damping disappears — “fade.” Bigger oil volume + nitrogen help fight
this. That’s why tube volume matters in SA conditions.
Ask us which EFS tier matches your weight and terrain.
