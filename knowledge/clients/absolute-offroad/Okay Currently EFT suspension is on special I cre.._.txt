🎯 Part 2: Low-Budget, Maximum-Impact Targeting Strategy

To get this in front of the correct people while minimizing spend, you must adopt a highly precise, low-funnel targeting strategy. Your target is the high-value, older, and technically-minded Land Cruiser owner ("The Purist") within a 50 km drive radius of Alberton.

1. Geo-Targeting: Hyper-Local Focus

Location: Set your primary target to a 25-30 km radius around your physical address: 28 St Columb Rd, New Redruth, Alberton.

Rationale: While you asked for 50km, a smaller radius focuses your limited budget on the highest intent audience who can easily drive to your fitment center. You are a local retailer, so drive foot traffic first.

Target: Select "People who live in this location" (highest relevance for local businesses).

Exclude/Exclude: You may consider excluding very high-competition areas or low-value areas after monitoring results, but for the initial boost, focus on inclusion.

2. Detailed Targeting: Precision over Volume

Focus on Interests and Behaviors that indicate both the wealth to afford an LC79/R40k suspension and the enthusiasm to modify it.

LC79 owners are less price-sensitive and prioritize reliability, indicating higher disposable income.

Demographics

Age: 35-65+ (The LC79 owner, "The Oom"). Gender: Male (Primary demographic).

Ensures you target the core, purchasing demographic.

3. Low-Budget Optimization

Small, Consistent Budget: Instead of one large boost, run the ad for a longer duration (e.g., 7 days) with a consistent daily budget (e.g., R150 - R250/day). Consistency helps Facebook's algorithm learn and optimize performance.

Ad Sets for Testing: If possible, split your daily budget across two separate ad sets to A/B test your audience.

Ad Set A (The Purist): Toyota Land Cruiser + Off-roading + 4x4 Accessories.

Ad Set B (The Adventurer): Ford Ranger + ARB 4x4 Accessories + Adventure Travel.

Boost the Right Post: Do not simply click the "Boost Post" button on your page. Use Meta Ads Manager and select the Lead Generation or Conversions objective. This is the only way to get the precise targeting and optimization required for a high-value sale.

Monitor and Reallocate: After 3 days, check the performance. If Ad Set A is generating cheaper leads (lower Cost Per Click/Lead), shift the remaining budget from Ad Set B to Ad Set A.

The single most effective action for a low-budget, high-value local ad is precise targeting combined with a strong, unique offer, and your EFS LC79 special provides both.
