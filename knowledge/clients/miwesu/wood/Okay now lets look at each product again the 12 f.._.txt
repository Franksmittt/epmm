RESIN & QUILL: The Indigenous Collection - Visual Directives

MASTER STYLE TOKENS (Append to all prompts)

Technique: Hyper-realistic macro photography, 100mm macro lens, f/2.8 depth of field, sharp focus on the subject, soft cinematic lighting, 8k resolution, Unreal Engine 5 render style.

Vibe: Old money, heritage craftsmanship, biological precision, expensive textures (brass, leadwood, glass, water).
